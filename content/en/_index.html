What is OpenDataology?

The mission of OpenDataology is to provide a crowd-sourced platform that provides approaches to analyze and document the license compliance risks of publicly available datasets used for Artificial Intelligence (AI) software. In addition, the project endeavors to develop and promote open standards that capture the metadata required for performing license compliance analysis, dataset license compliance analysis processes and supporting tools.

Datasets Compliance Process

OpenDataology provides a dataset license compliance analysis workflow that ascertains the final allowed rights and the required obligations associated with using a publicly available dataset or a dataset that is curated from multiple data sources for any purpose. Please refer to the Paper .

License Tools & Service

OpenDataology has launched a crowdsourcing project - Datasets Metadata Portal, aimed at extracting license, copyright information from numerous open datasets. These information can help data users understand whether the source of the data is reliable and whether the data is used compliantly. A License Generator is also provided for data publishers, help them choose a good data license. Due to the Private Policy, we do not collect any personal data.

Open Standard

OpenDataology opendataology is currently working closely with the SPDX communityand its AI BOM standard. We are now working together, building the open standards for AI datasets in areas such as compliance, security and privacy protection.


Technologies

A Human-Machine Readable Metadata Model will be used for describe the AI dataset and its license information. The Data Clone Detection will be used for dataset lineage.

Best Practice
